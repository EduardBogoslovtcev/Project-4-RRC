Games:

A small featured collection of some of Solgryn's previous games. The most notable one being I Wanna Be The Boshy, which kickstarted all of Grynsoft. Its popularity brought Grynsoft's first original game Wings of Vi into the limelight.

Wings of Vi

Wings of Vi is a challenging 2D action platformer developed and published by Grynsoft, released for Windows on November 28, 2014. You play as Vi, an angel on a divine quest to defeat the Demon Lord Jeh’oul, who has been unleashed upon the world.

I wanna be the Boshy

"I Wanna Be the Boshy" is a notoriously difficult fan-made 2D platformer. It was released in 2010, and it's one of the most well-known entries in the "I Wanna Be the Guy"-inspired genre of brutally hard platformers.

Blob’s Adventure

Blob’s Adventure is an action game developed by Solgryn using Multimedia Fusion 2.0, released in 2009. It was later archived on Kliktopia in 2021
